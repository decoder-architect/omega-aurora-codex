The Ω‑Aurora Network proposes a global standard to federate ethical education and computational transparency across academic, research, and industry domains.

- **Mission:** To create a universally auditable infrastructure for intelligent systems, linking ethical learning and computational accountability in all settings.
- **Motivation:**
  - The rapid expansion of AI capabilities risks value drift and opacity in reasoning.
  - A distributed, human-governed audit network enables collective oversight, transparent learning, and cross‑disciplinary collaboration.
  - Ethical literacy and technical advancement must progress together for social benefit.
