**Three Pillars of Measurement**

1. **Impact Performance** – quantitative metrics from ledger data & policy adoption.  
2. **Ethical Integrity** – qualitative assessment via peer review and audits.  
3. **Cultural Continuity** – sustainability of education and public trust.  

Each pillar is reviewed by a distinct board within the Ω‑Council’s Evaluation Division.


**Step Intervals:**

***

### **V. METHODOLOGY**

**Quantitative Assessment**
- Extract aggregated metrics from Ω‑Ledger (Integrity, Compassion, Transparency).  
- Normalize through cross‑regional variance index.  
- Compute Global Ethical Performance Score (GEPS):  
  $$
  GEPS = \frac{(Integrity + Compassion + Transparency)}{3}
  $$

**Qualitative Assessment**
- Randomized peer reviews of five ledger entries per node per year.  
- Evaluation rubric (clarity of reflection, depth of learning, cultural context).  
- Public case‑study repository tagged by theme and region.  

***

### **VI. REPORTING AND TRANSPARENCY**

- **Aurora Integrity Report:** comprehensive annual publication (EN + FR + other languages).  
- **Open Dashboard:** live display of regional Ethical Flux Indices.  
- **Audit Repository:** searchable JSON‑LD ledger available under CC BY‑SA.  
- **Independent Oversight:** rotation committee selected every two years to review methodology.  

All data undertake triple verification before release (public node signature + Council stamp + checksum).
