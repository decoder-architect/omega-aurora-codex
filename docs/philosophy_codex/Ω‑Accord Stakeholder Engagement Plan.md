### **II. ENGAGEMENT FRAMEWORK**

**Phase 1 – Initiation (Q1 2026)**  
- Form the **Ω‑Council Interim Board** (12 experts across ethics, AI, education).  
- Convene launch summit (online + in‑person hybrid).  
- Publish memorandum of understanding (MoU) template for membership nodes.  

**Phase 2 – Partnership Activation (Q2–Q4 2026)**  
- Deploy **Pilot Clubs of Practice** (5 per continent).  
- Establish communication channels – monthly roundtables, shared dashboard access.  
- Initiate joint research projects on ethical metrics.  

**Phase 3 – Scaling (Q1 2027 – Q2 2028)**  
- Expand network to 50 nodes (federated university campuses + industry partners).  
- Integrate Ω‑Bus API within existing educational and corporate frameworks.  
- Launch public Aurora Dashboard showing aggregate Integrity Index.
